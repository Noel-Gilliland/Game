#### Guide to How Character progression will work. 
Option 1: Each class will have a skill tree they can progress to unlock new abilities and passives, making their character stronger. To unlock a skill, you must pay a certain currency, a boss trophy of some kind? Each major boss you defeat will award you with a certain number of points to use. This is how 
you can progress the game by purchasing the zone required abilities. The structure of each tree will be made in a way such that, the progression of the game can differ based on the character you play, but will force you to buy a zone ability to progress the game, before your allowed others. Due to this being 
a major flaw point, if you somehow found a way to avoid buying the neccessary ones, you can always for free reset this skill tree, other than the major abilities. 

Option 2: Each class has a trainer of some kind that you can find in the world, and bring back to whitesparrow. Then, you can purchase new skills for you character but like, non progression based ones. Progression based ones will simply be tied to bosses or locations like most games.
